Feature Details

Company Stock Management (Database-Based)

Handles ownership, purchase/sale transactions and investor profiles. Uses normalized relational tables and transaction logs for transparency and auditability.

Audit Report Management (Database-Based)

Catalogues reports with metadata, implements versioning and user-based access controls. SQL triggers automate archiving and updates.

Trade Management (Database-Based)

Records trade events, enforces ACID properties, validates via stored procedures and exposes views for reporting.

AI-Based Fraud Detection (AI-Based)

Supervised and unsupervised models analyze historical transactions to detect anomalies and generate alerts for investigation.

Price Monitoring (AI-Based)

Continuously monitors price feeds and news sentiment using time-series analysis to surface early warnings of volatility.

Prediction of Stock Price (DB + AI Integration)

Historical data pipelines feed ML models; predictions are stored for visualization and decision support.
